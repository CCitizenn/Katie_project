My Dearest Bebe

The hour is late, and the gaslights in the street below have long since flickered into a dull, amber haze...
Is how I would love to be able to start this letter, however, I am not a 19th century novelist. This website was made for you, to show you how much you mean to me, and how grateful I am to be apart of your life. I tried personalise and tailor this as much as possible, to make it a special experience just for you, but, in all honesty, some of these ideas are inspired from things I've seen online. I took my own twist on it, and made it all myself though.

I made this only partially because I messed up the christmas presents, but more wholly because I wanted to, as projects in the name of you feel meaningful to me. This is the type of thing I hope you can brag about to your friends, and the type of thing you can look at whenever you feel sad, or lonely, or like the whole world is against you. This is a declaration that you are loved, and cherished, and adored. This is reassurance that no matter how you feel, there is a man here willing to feel it with you. I hope this counter never stops counting up, and the gallery keeps growing.

Yours always,
Liam Golzar
